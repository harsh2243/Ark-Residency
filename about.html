Our Story

A calm, practical hotel for business and leisure guests.

Ark Residency keeps the experience simple: check in quickly, settle into a clean room, and rely on a team that is ready to help when needed. That makes the property suitable for short city visits as well as longer stays.

The hotel sits in Andheri West, one of Mumbai’s most convenient and connected neighbourhoods, with easy access to business districts, entertainment spots, and airport routes.

Every detail is focused on everyday comfort rather than unnecessary decoration, so the page feels familiar, trustworthy, and standard in the way guests expect from a hotel website.
