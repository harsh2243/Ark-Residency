About Ark Residency Hotels

Ark Residency is a modern hotel built for guests who value comfort, convenience, and a reliable stay experience in Mumbai. We focus on keeping everything simple, from room selection to guest support.

Strategically located in Andheri West, the hotel connects easily to business districts, entertainment hubs, and the airport. That makes it suitable for corporate travelers, families, and guests visiting for short or extended stays.

Our approach is straightforward: clean rooms, responsive service, and practical facilities that help every guest feel settled quickly.
